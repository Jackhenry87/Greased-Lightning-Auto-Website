Our Story

Built on Trust,
Not Upsells

Greased Lightning Service Center has been serving the Fort Worth and River Oaks community for over three decades. Owner David Foreman built this shop on one simple idea: treat people right, charge them fairly, and they'll come back for life.

Head mechanic Brian has been turning wrenches since the '80s, and customers have followed him here from across the Metroplex — some driving all the way from Granbury — just for his honest diagnosis and fair pricing. When Brian tells you what's wrong with your car, you can take it to the bank.

No frills. No gimmicks. No upsells. Just good people doing good work at prices that make sense.
